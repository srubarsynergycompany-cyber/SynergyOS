# Codex Project Context

## Project
SynergyOS is the operating system for Synergy Company and future Synergy AI products.

## Main Workstreams
- SynergyOS: fulfillment ERP and operational platform.
- Synergy Web: customer acquisition website.
- Synergy AI: reusable AI platform and future SaaS product.

## Current Priority
Build Synergy AI as a platform layer, not only as one internal chatbot.

## Working Rules
- Work step by step.
- Keep changes small and reviewable.
- Do not change production behavior without approval.
- Prefer documentation and architecture before risky implementation.
- Sensitive actions require approval.

## Synergy AI Direction
The first AI module is AI Director.
It coordinates specialist assistants, plans work, summarizes status and prepares safe recommendations.
