# Synergy AI Business Model

## Purpose
Define the commercial logic for turning Synergy AI from an internal assistant system into a sellable SaaS platform.

## Product Positioning
Synergy AI is a platform for AI employees/modules that help companies manage sales, marketing, operations, finance, support and internal knowledge.

It should not be positioned as a generic chatbot. It should be positioned as an AI operating layer for business execution.

## Target Customers

### Initial Target
- fulfillment companies,
- e-commerce companies,
- small and medium businesses with repeated operational workflows,
- companies that need sales and marketing automation but do not have large internal teams.

### Later Target
- manufacturing companies,
- logistics companies,
- agencies,
- accounting firms,
- service businesses.

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Core Platform License
Base monthly license for access to Synergy AI Platform.

### 2. Assistant Modules
Paid specialist AI modules.

Examples:
- Sales AI,
- Marketing AI,
- Finance AI,
- Operations AI,
- Customer Support AI.

### 3. Integrations
Paid connectors to external systems.

Examples:
- e-shop platforms,
- carriers,
- CRM,
- accounting,
- Google Workspace,
- GitHub.

### 4. Implementation Fee
One-time setup fee for company onboarding, data preparation and custom assistant configuration.

### 5. Enterprise Customization
Higher-priced custom implementation for larger clients.

## Possible Pricing Structure

### Starter
For small companies testing AI workflows.

### Growth
For growing companies using multiple assistants and integrations.

### Pro
For companies using AI across departments.

### Enterprise
Custom pricing, advanced permissions, integrations and support.

## Competitive Advantage
- modular AI assistant structure,
- business-specific workflows,
- integration with operational systems,
- auditability,
- human approval model,
- future SynergyOS integration,
- internal validation in real fulfillment operations.

## Go-To-Market Strategy
1. Validate internally inside Synergy Fulfillment.
2. Build demo use cases in SynergyOS.
3. Prepare pilot offering for selected companies.
4. Sell first implementations manually.
5. Convert repeated patterns into standard modules.
6. Scale through SaaS packaging.

## First Commercial Hypothesis
The first paid product should focus on companies that need help with sales, marketing and operations but cannot afford full specialist teams.

The value proposition:
"Activate an AI team that helps your company sell more, manage operations better and reduce repetitive work."
