# Synergy AI Roadmap

## Roadmap Goal
Build Synergy AI as a reusable AI platform that supports internal company operations, integrates with SynergyOS and can later be sold as a SaaS product to other companies.

## Phase 1 – Documentation Foundation

### Goal
Create the strategic and technical foundation before implementation starts.

### Deliverables
- AI_MASTER_PLAN.md
- AI_ARCHITECTURE.md
- AGENTS.md
- ROADMAP.md
- KNOWLEDGE_BASE.md
- SECURITY.md
- AUTOMATIONS.md
- MARKETPLACE.md
- BUSINESS_MODEL.md

### Success Criteria
- The purpose of the platform is clear.
- AI roles are defined.
- Permissions are defined.
- Commercial direction is documented.
- First MVP scope is agreed.

---

## Phase 2 – Internal MVP

### Goal
Create the first usable internal AI Manager for Synergy.

### Initial Scope
- AI workspace inside SynergyOS or as a connected internal module.
- AI Director as the first assistant.
- Read-only knowledge base.
- Manual human approval for all external actions.
- Support for project planning, documentation, sales drafts and operational analysis.

### Success Criteria
- User can ask internal business questions.
- AI can reference documented project knowledge.
- AI can prepare but not execute sensitive actions.
- Workflows are logged.

---

## Phase 3 – Specialist Assistants

### Goal
Add the first specialist assistants and route work through AI Director.

### Assistants
- Sales AI
- Marketing AI
- Operations AI
- Finance AI
- Development AI

### Success Criteria
- Each assistant has a clear role.
- AI Director can delegate tasks.
- Outputs are structured and comparable.
- Conflicting recommendations are escalated.

---

## Phase 4 – SynergyOS Integration

### Goal
Connect Synergy AI to real SynergyOS data.

### Integrations
- orders,
- clients,
- products,
- stock,
- carriers,
- returns,
- claims,
- reporting.

### Success Criteria
- AI can analyze operational data.
- AI can recommend warehouse improvements.
- AI can draft client-facing explanations.
- Audit log records all AI activity.

---

## Phase 5 – Automation Layer

### Goal
Allow safe execution of selected low-risk tasks.

### Initial Automations
- daily summaries,
- sales follow-up reminders,
- draft emails,
- report generation,
- documentation updates,
- task creation.

### Success Criteria
- Low-risk actions can run automatically.
- Sensitive actions require approval.
- All automation results are auditable.

---

## Phase 6 – Multi-Tenant Platform

### Goal
Prepare Synergy AI for external customers.

### Required Capabilities
- tenant isolation,
- tenant-specific knowledge bases,
- tenant-specific assistants,
- role-based access control,
- billing-ready module structure,
- onboarding flow,
- audit logs per tenant.

### Success Criteria
- One platform can safely serve multiple companies.
- Company-specific data cannot leak between tenants.
- Assistant modules can be enabled/disabled per tenant.

---

## Phase 7 – Commercial Launch

### Goal
Package Synergy AI as a sellable product.

### Deliverables
- pricing model,
- landing page,
- demo environment,
- onboarding checklist,
- support process,
- first pilot customers.

### Success Criteria
- External company can start using the platform.
- First paid pilot is possible.
- Product value is clear without custom explanation.

---
